FarsiReads

FarsiReads is a Farsi Platform to provide the good and selected Farsi Reads(e.g. Books,Articles, Blog posts, etc.) for farsi-speaking people.

It is social-based and interactive in a sense to encourage the reading in general and also promote the valuable Christian works. Its ambitious aim is to develop or even create the vital habit of reading which is absent in majority of the farsi-speakers including both Christians and non-christians alike.

Books

In this section a collection of farsi Christian books and booklets including the original farsi works as well as translated ones are gathered in a database. You can search to see whether a specific topic is available in farsi and see a short summary of the book. You could also browse the books using the book categories which are sorted by topics. In this section we choose and analyse and recommend a book monthly; you could also be informed about the new titles which are translated or published. Using this section enables you to establish and organize your own virtual library to keep track of your already read and current reading books. The main purpose is to organize the complete collection of all farsi Christian works regardless of their confessions.
